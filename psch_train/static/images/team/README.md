# Team Photos

Place your team member photos in this folder with the following filenames:

- `shakthi.jpg` - Shakthi G's photo
- `sanushia.jpg` - Sanushia L's photo
- `saranya.jpg` - Saranya B's photo
- `sharmily.jpg` - Sharmily M's photo

## Recommended Specifications

- **Size**: 400x400 pixels (square aspect ratio works best)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Quality**: Good resolution for the circular frame display

The photos will be displayed in circular frames with a grayscale effect that transitions to full color on hover.

If a photo is missing, a placeholder icon will be shown instead.
