OUR MISSION

We are a multidisciplinary student team passionate about combining Artificial Intelligence and Psychology.

Our mission is to create a safe, intelligent training environment where psychology students can practice clinical skills using AI-simulated patients before entering real-world practice. Like entering the Upside Down, our platform allows students to explore challenging scenarios in a controlled dimension where mistakes become learning opportunities.
